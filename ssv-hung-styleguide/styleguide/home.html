KK GLASS is a major independent glass merchant in the country and arguably one of Australia’s top suppliers of performance glass products.

Since 1993 to today KK Glass has continued to grow, expand and evolve the business from a specialist wholesale and merchandiser serving local glass companies to major independent glass merchant with global processing plants, sophisticated local storage facility and providing high-quality value-add glass products.

KK GLASS aims to be the leading provider of premium glass and glazing solutions by delivering cutting-edge processing capabilities, high quality products and excellence in customer service.
